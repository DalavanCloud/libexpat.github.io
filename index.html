Welcome to Expat!

Welcome to Expat, a stream-oriented XML parser library written in C.

Please note, this website is work-in-progress. For now, www.libexpat.org may be of more help. Be encouraged to join the transition with your own pull requests.

Expat excels

  • with files that do not fit RAM, and
  • where performance and flexibility are crucial.

If you would rather use a DOM parser, you may want to check out libxml2.
